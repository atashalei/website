WELCOME TO FAMY NATIONAL INTEGRATED HIGH SCHOOL

HISTORY OF SCHOOL

Famy National Integrated High School, which opened in 1969, has been a cornerstone of education in its community for 55 years. Since its humble beginnings, the school has developed tremendously, adjusting to the changing educational landscape while remaining dedicated to offering outstanding education. Over the years, hundreds of students have passed through its halls, armed with information and abilities that have enabled them to flourish in a variety of disciplines. and continues to uphold its heritage of academic excellence and community involvement, creating new generations with the same dedication and enthusiasm as when it first opened its doors.

LOCATION

Famy National Integrated High School (FNIHS) is located in Barangay Tunhac 2, Famy, Laguna. FNIHS is zl-known for its commitment to offering outstanding education and supporting student growth. It also plays an important role in the community. The school is located in a tranquil area of Laguna, providing it an ideal setting for learning and growth. Its strategic location makes it easily accessible to students from Famy and the neighboring areas.

POPULATION

It have a total of 2418 students, with 1256 males and 1162 females attending.
